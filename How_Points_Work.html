Why Every Day Matters

Your brain learns better in small daily doses

Scientists have studied how memory works for over 100 years, and the results are clear: short daily sessions beat long cramming sessions every time.

The Spacing Effect

Reviewing material across multiple days creates 2-3x stronger memories than studying the same amount in one sitting. Your brain literally builds stronger connections while you sleep after each session.

Active Recall & Immediate Feedback

After learning something new, you forget 70% within 24 hours unless you practice retrieving it. Our AI-powered sessions give you comprehension questions with instant feedback, forcing your brain to recall and solidify knowledge in the moment — and every time you practice, the memory gets stronger.

Reading & Comprehension

Reading 20 minutes a day exposes you to 1.8 million words per year. That amount of input builds vocabulary, critical thinking, and test performance in ways that can't be crammed.

Featured Task

Today's Session: AI-Powered Learning

The heart of your daily routine is Today's Session (also called "The Archive" or "Mission Briefing"). It's a fresh, personalized learning experience generated just for you.

How It Works

1. Pick a topic. Three choices appear based on your interests and what you're studying. Want history, science, current events? You choose.
2. Read the passage. AI generates a 600-800 word piece at your exact grade level — it's always fresh, never repetitive.
3. Answer questions. Four comprehension questions test whether you actually understood the material.
4. Get instant feedback. See your score immediately and review any questions you got wrong.

Why this beats traditional studying: The questions force active recall — your brain has to retrieve the information you just read, which is way more powerful than just re-reading. Combined with immediate feedback, your brain locks in knowledge fast. That's science.

Once per week (Wednesdays): Instead of just questions, a writing prompt appears. You write a response, get AI-powered feedback on content and writing quality, and earn 3 points when you submit.

Khan Academy connection: The system also shows you a targeted Khan Academy lesson recommendation for that day — something related to what you're working on. Click it to do your 2-pt Khan task.

Questions
When do I get paid?
Dad pays every Sunday evening via Apple Pay. Other contributors pay on their own schedule, but every payout is tracked on your dashboard.
Do my points go away after payout?
No. Points are your lifetime score — they never go down. Only the dollar balance resets after you're paid.
What's "Today's Session" and how does it work?
Today's Session is our AI-powered learning experience. The system picks a topic from 3 choices based on your interests, generates a fresh 600-800 word passage at your grade level, and you answer 4 comprehension questions with instant feedback. Get 75-99% correct for +1 bonus point, 100% for +2. It's designed for active recall — the questions force you to think critically about what you just read.
What if I miss a day?
You lose your streak, which means no streak bonus until you build it back up. But you can still do bonus sessions on other days to hit your 21/21 weekly target.
Can I do bonus sessions to make up for missed days?
Bonus sessions count toward your weekly credits (21/21), so they can save your Perfect Week bonus. But they can't save a broken streak — that requires consecutive days. Writing responses (Wednesdays) are separate and don't count toward 21/21, but they're worth 3 pts each when you complete them.
What happens if I miss the diagnostic test window?
If you don't complete the test within the ~1 week window, it expires and you earn zero diagnostic points for that month. But your daily points and streaks are unaffected — you just miss the bonus opportunity. A new test will come the following month.
Do I still need to do my daily tasks on diagnostic test day?
No. Completing the diagnostic test automatically marks Today's Session, Khan, and Reading as done for that day. You earn 15 points total (10 base + 5 bonus) — more than a normal perfect day of 10 points.
Is there a maximum I can earn?
No hard cap. The more you do, the more you earn. Bonus sessions are unlimited. A great month could earn $120-$150+ from Dad alone — plus whatever other contributors add.
What do I buy with my money?
Anything you used to ask Dad to buy: game skins, in-app purchases, snacks, movies, stuff on Amazon. Dad still covers school supplies, clothes, haircuts, sports, and family meals.
Does money pass through Dad from other contributors?
No. Each person pays you directly — Apple Pay, Venmo, cash, however they choose. Dad doesn't handle anyone else's contributions. It goes straight from them to you.
What if I don't get 100% on Today's Session comprehension questions?
You still earn the full 3 pts for completing the session. If you score 75-99%, you earn +1 comprehension bonus. Only perfect scores (100%) earn the full +2 bonus. Either way, the feedback helps you learn from any mistakes.
When do I write my weekly response?
Every Wednesday. A writing prompt appears on your dashboard with AI evaluation built in. You get instant feedback on your writing, and 3 points are added to your account once you submit. It's designed to build critical thinking and writing skills.
Why does daily earn so much more than cramming?
Because your brain actually learns better in small daily doses. The reward system matches the science: daily practice with immediate feedback builds real knowledge AND earns real money. Cramming does neither well.
